Bankiom - Fintech Super-App

AI Integration Design Systems User Journey Mapping

The Challenge

The client's vision was a revolutionary fintech "super-app" combining budgeting, investments, and AI-powered financial advice. The central UX challenge was immense: how to unify these complex, data-heavy services into a single, cohesive experience that felt simple, empowering, and trustworthy to the average user.

Understanding the User

Persona: The Novice Investor

Age: 25-35

Goal: Start investing but is intimidated by complex platforms.

Pain Point: Fears making a costly mistake due to lack of knowledge.

Persona: The Busy Professional

Age: 35-50

Goal: Optimize finances without spending too much time.

Pain Point: Uses multiple apps for banking, budgeting, and investing.

From Sketch to Screen

Mapping out the user flow and core app structure.

Design & Prototyping

A modular design system was crucial. We used a clean, card-based UI with clear data visualizations to make complex information digestible. I designed the AI interface to provide recommendations with clear reasoning and sliders for users to adjust risk levels, giving them a sense of control.
