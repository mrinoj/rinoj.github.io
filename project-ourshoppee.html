Conversion Optimization A/B Testing UI Design

The Problem

A successful e-commerce brand faced a classic "leaky funnel": high traffic but a low conversion rate of just 1.5%. Analytics pointed to a high cart abandonment rate, with the multi-page checkout process being the primary culprit.

01. Discovery & Research

Using Hotjar, I analyzed heatmaps and session recordings of over 200 user sessions. I discovered that users were dropping off significantly when asked to create an account and when presented with unexpected shipping costs late in the process.

02. Design & Prototyping

I designed two primary challengers in Figma for A/B testing: one with a prominent guest checkout and another with a collapsible one-page accordion layout. Both designs brought shipping information forward in the flow.

03. Testing & Iteration

We ran the A/B tests over two weeks. The version with the prominent guest checkout and accordion layout was the clear winner, showing a statistically significant lift in conversions.
