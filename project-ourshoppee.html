OurShoppee - E-Commerce Experience

Conversion Optimization A/B Testing UI Design

The Problem

A successful e-commerce brand faced a classic "leaky funnel" problem: high traffic but a low conversion rate of just 1.5%. Analytics and session recordings pointed to a high cart abandonment rate, with the multi-page checkout process being the primary culprit.

My Role

As the lead UI/UX designer, I aimed to plug the leaks and drive revenue. I was responsible for analyzing user behavior, forming data-driven hypotheses, and designing and running A/B tests to validate solutions.

The Process

Discovery & Research: Using Hotjar, I analyzed heatmaps and session recordings of over 200 user sessions. I discovered that users were dropping off significantly when asked to create an account and when presented with unexpected shipping costs late in the process.

Design & Prototyping: I designed two primary challengers in Figma for A/B testing: one with a prominent guest checkout and another with a collapsible one-page accordion layout. Both designs brought shipping information forward in the flow.

Testing & Iteration: We ran the A/B tests over two weeks. The version with the prominent guest checkout and accordion layout was the clear winner, showing a statistically significant lift in conversions.

The Outcome & Impact

The results were immediate and impactful. By implementing the winning design, the site's overall conversion rate increased by 25%, and the average order value saw a 15% uplift in the first quarter alone. The project delivered a clear return on investment and established a new, data-driven design process for the company.
